# Other Mods/Utilities

In addition to the mods in our list, here are some other mods/utilities that live outside of the launcher:
- **TeamRuns** - Jak 1 Multiplayer! Play locally or online P2P with your friends in co-op or head-to-head modes! [Website](https://teamruns.web.app/)
- **CustomJak** - Blender plugin for creating custom Jak skins for Jak 1! [Download](https://github.com/Kuitar5/customJak/releases/latest) / [Instructions](https://youtu.be/cpvJ3m8Otrc)
- **Jak Tracker** - Customizable UI that automatically tracks collected cells (inspired by OOT Randomizer trackers). [Download](https://github.com/OpenGOAL-Unofficial-Mods/jak-tracker/releases/latest) / [Instructions](https://github.com/OpenGOAL-Unofficial-Mods/jak-tracker/blob/main/README.md)
- **OpenGOAL-Platform-Tools** - Blender plugin for laying out platforms and paths [Website](https://github.com/gratefulforest/OpenGOAL-Platform-Tools)
- **OpenGOAL-Navmesh-Tools** - Blender plugin for designing nav-meshes [Website](https://github.com/gratefulforest/OpenGOAL-Navmesh-Tools)
